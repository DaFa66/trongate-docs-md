# How To Get Involved

## Support Trongate's Growth


GitHub stars are a simple yet powerful way to show your support for Trongate. They help increase the framework's visibility and credibility within the developer community.


[Give Trongate a Star on GitHub](https://github.com/trongate/trongate-framework)

## Other Ways To Contribute


Trongate is an open-source framework that thrives on community collaboration. We encourage you to participate by reporting issues, proposing features, or submitting bug fixes. All contributions, regardless of size, are greatly valued, and pull requests are always welcome.

### Contribute to the Framework


The Trongate framework is hosted on GitHub, where all contributions to its core development are managed. If you’re interested in enhancing the framework, please explore the repository and submit your improvements:


[Contribute to the Trongate Framework on GitHub](https://github.com/trongate/trongate-framework)

### Help Improve the Documentation


The Trongate documentation is a critical resource for our community, and we invite contributions to ensure it remains accurate, comprehensive, and current. If you identify errors, gaps, or opportunities to enhance the documentation, please consider sharing your expertise:


[Contribute to the Trongate Documentation on GitHub](https://github.com/trongate/trongate-docs)

### Contribute to the YouTube Channel


A dedicated YouTube channel has been established to offer tutorials, in-depth feature explorations, and insights into the Trongate framework. If you’re interested in creating video content or sharing your knowledge, please contact us to discuss collaboration opportunities:


[Contact Form for YouTube Channel Contributions](https://trongate.io/contactus)

### Spread the Word About Trongate


Raising awareness about Trongate is essential to its growth and adoption. We encourage you to share your experiences with the framework through various platforms, including:


- Writing articles or blog posts
- Creating video tutorials or reviews on platforms like YouTube
- Discussing Trongate on podcasts or within developer communities
- Recommending Trongate to fellow developers and clients
- Mentioning Trongate on social media platforms such as [X.com](https://x.com/home)


Your efforts to promote Trongate are deeply appreciated and significantly contribute to its success. No permission is required to share your enthusiasm for the framework.

## Your Support Matters


Every contribution, whether it's code, documentation, or simply spreading the word, helps shape the future of Trongate. Together, we can build a robust, stable, and innovative PHP framework for generations to come.
